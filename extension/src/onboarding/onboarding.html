Open tabs in any Chrome profile with one click.

1

Install the helper app

Profilissimo needs a small helper to communicate with Chrome profiles. Open a terminal and run:

curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/OWNER/profilissimo/main/installer/install.sh | bash
curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/OWNER/profilissimo/main/installer/install.sh | bash
irm https://raw.githubusercontent.com/OWNER/profilissimo/main/installer/install.ps1 | iex
2

Restart Chrome

After installing, quit Chrome completely (Cmd+Q) and reopen it. Chrome only detects new native apps on startup.

3

Verify connection

Click the button below to check that everything is working.

✓

You're all set!

Profilissimo is connected and ready to use. Click the extension icon in your toolbar to transfer tabs between profiles.
